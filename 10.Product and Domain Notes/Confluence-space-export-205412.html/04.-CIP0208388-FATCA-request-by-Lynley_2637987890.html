  • What is an NFFE?

An entity that is not classified under the definitions of an FFI (foreign financial institution) is considered an NFFE (Non-Financial Foreign Entity)

 

  • What is FDAP Income?

FDAP is an abbreviation for Fixed, Determinable, Annual or Periodic income. FDAP income applies to foreign persons earning income from US sources. Such persons will be subject to 30% withholding tax or a lower rate if there is a tax treaty between the United States and the country of residency.

  • What is a Recalcitrant Account Holder? >>

Uncooperative, non comply??

A recalcitrant account holder is any account holder that:

  • Fails to comply with reasonable requests for information necessary to determine if the account is a United States account;

  • Fails to provide the name, address, and Tax Identification Number (TIN) of each “specified United States person” and each substantial United States owner of a US-owned foreign entity; or

  • Fails to provide a waiver of any foreign law that would prevent a foreign financial institution from reporting information required under FATCA.

Purpose: This stored procedure retrieves account details required for FATCA and CRS tax regimes.

It processes account data from CRM,

filters it into (FATCA, FATCA_CRS and CRS),

and finally inserts the required information into the output tables for reporting.

Parameters

  1. @PeriodStartDate - The start date of the period to consider.

  2. @PeriodEndDate - The end date of the period to consider.

  3. @TRReportingDate - The final date for tax reporting date.

  4. @OperationType - The type of operation defined by the US tax reporting Standard.

  5. @RefreshHoldingsData - A flag indicating whether to refresh holding data (0 or 1).

Main Sections:

What is the Criterial for NFFEs??

  1. Substantial Controlling Interest:

    • The entity must have a substantial controlling interest (ce.[SubstantialControllingInterest] = 1 >> this is a flag from CRM).

  2. FATCA Regime Exclusion:

    • The entity should not be under the 'FATCA' regime (NOT (ce.Regime = 'FATCA')).

  3. CIP ISO Code:

    • The entity must have a non-null CIP ISO code (cc.ft_cip_ISOcode IS NOT NULL).

  4. Active Status:

    • The entity must be active (sm2.value = 'Active').

  5. Foreign Tax Resident or US Tax Identification:

    • The entity must either:

      • Be a foreign tax resident (ISNULL(a.dsl_foreigntaxresident, '') = 1).

      • OR >> Have a US tax identification (ISNULL(a.dsl_USCitizensforTaxPurposes, 2) = 1).

  6. Other Conditions:

    • Entities marked as active with no FT (Foreign Tax) flags set (OR ISNULL(sm2.value, '') = 'Active'). >> sm2 is the mapping of “dsl_passive or Active“
